Track the Garbage Truck!

WHEN

Our tracking system confirms that waste collection is scheduled for every Sunday and Thursday at 10:00 AM. Check the map to see when the truck is approaching your street and ensure your bags are ready for a seamless pickup.

WHERE

We've mapped out every metal mesh waste cage in the community to prevent illegal dumping and keep our sidewalks clean. Residents are encouraged to drop off their secured bags only at these verified designated points.

HOW

To keep our community green, please separate your waste into three categories: Biodegradable, Non-Biodegradable, and E-Waste. Verified local guidelines require all electronics and batteries to be set aside safely.
